Featured Projects

ML Appications to Pure-Play Online Auto Lender Case Study

Built an ML pricing engine for online auto-loans that boosts expected revenue per offer from $551 to $683 (+24 %) with a flat 5.1 % APR, then personalises rates via a Random-Forest sweep to add $83.9 k (+42 %) on the test set. Because refinance applicants proved markedly less APR-sensitive, the model flags an extra 50–100 bp of safe margin on those loans.

Emotion Recognition in Unity

Unity program delivering real-time facial-emotion recognition: webcam frames are face-aligned via OpenCV landmarks, preprocessed to handle rotation/scale, then passed to a deep convolution neural networks trained on the FER+ dataset, and deployed through Unity’s Barracuda inference engine to accurately classify each face into one of eight emotions.
