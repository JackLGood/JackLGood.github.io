About Me

I am a junior studying Mathematics and Computer Science through the College Scholars program at Cornell University, where I'm fortunate to be advised by Abe Davis. I'm doing research at the intersection of graphics and machine learning, focusing on physical simulations and new ways of representing real-world objects in digital form.

In my spare time, I love exploring the outdoors, reading sci-fi novels, and writing short stories on computational physics. Check out the rest of this site for my publications, experiences, and personal projects.
