Current Projects

Real-Time Face Recognition and Probability Prediction System

I live in a fraternity and one of the rooms sees constant “hanging out” and I noticed not only do some people come by much more than others, but the times they arrive and leave are significant. Fraternity guys are lazy, so wouldn’t it be nice to predict when people come to hang out in a minimally invasive way? I’m developing a real-time facial recognition system that identifies individuals as they enter and calculates the probability of their appearance based on historical data. Built with DeepFace and OpenCV, it processes live feeds, matches faces to a known database, and logs entry events. The system learns temporal patterns to predict attendance, designed to run locally or on a Raspberry Pi for smart‐environment automation.
