Featured Projects

ML Appications to Pure-Play Online Auto Lender Case Study

Designed and delivered an ML pricing engine for a targeted online auto-loan segment. A log-regression baseline showed current pricing yields $550.55 expected revenue per offer. Mapping the APR/revenue curve revealed that a flat 5.1 % APR lifts revenue to $682.96 (+24 %). I then engineered six predictive features and tuned a Random Forest with 0.89 validation accuracy; running per-applicant APR sweeps with this model added $83.9k (+42 %) incremental revenue on the test portfolio. The pipeline is modular, explainable (feature importance & SHAP), version-controlled.

Emotion Recognition in Unity

A Unity demo showing real-time facial emotion recognition using the FER+ dataset, Deep Convolution Neural Network, and Unity’s Barracuda inference engine. Webcam frames are face-aligned via OpenCV landmarks and fed into the network to detect one of eight emotions.
